ABOUT US

We're uplifting your spirits since the Christmas of 1909


ESTD. 1909

Ridora is a new contemporary blend, named to honour the memory of a whiskey-maker whose distillery was in its time the largest in Asia. Ridora and Co helped build the golden era of Asian whiskey in the 19th century. Our distilleries in the triad of China, South Korea, and the Philippines extended over 17 acres and we are Asia's largest exporter of whiskey. Ridora is the biggest name at the heart of Asia's historic brewing and distilling quarter.

Whisky Blending Traditions

Ridora Whiskey embodies the harmony that exists between Asian natural landscapes and the warmth of the Asian people. This unique fusion establishes Ridora Whiskey as a spiritual art form.

Philosophy and Quality

The best Asian artisans never add nor take away; only heightening the beauty and spirit that lives deep within every wheat ingredient. They are humble enough to acknowledge that their art can only be as great as the intrinsic richness of each ingredient. There is a surprising humility within their art, whether in the blending or the distillation. At every stage in creating the whiskey, this relationship between their craft, nature and humility inspires their uncompromising pursuit of taste and quality.
